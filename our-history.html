Our History

The origins of Mahatma Gandhi University of Medical Sciences and Technology (MGUMST) trace back to the establishment of Mahatma Gandhi Hospital in Jaipur.

Founded in year 2000, Mahatma Gandhi Hospital was conceived as a healthcare institution dedicated to providing compassionate and high-quality medical services to the community. Recognizing the need for comprehensive medical education to complement the hospital services, Mahatma Gandhi Medical College, Jaipur was founded in 2001. This marked the institution's commitment to nurturing the next generation of healthcare professionals and contributing to the field of medical sciences. After the achievement of Mahatma Gandhi Medical College and Hospital, the Mahatma Gandhi Dental College & Hospital was founded in 2003. Subsequently, in the same year, Mahatma Gandhi Nursing College was established, and in 2005, Mahatma Gandhi Physiotherapy College was also set up.

As our colleges continued to flourish, the visionaries behind the institution aspired for greater heights. In 2011, Mahatma Gandhi University of Medical Sciences and Technology officially came into existence, consolidating the hospital and medical college under one academic umbrella. This transition marked a significant milestone, signifying the institution's evolution into a comprehensive university.

Under the umbrella of MGUMST, the institution continued to grow and diversify its academic and research offerings. The university became a hub for medical education, research, and healthcare services, attracting students and faculty from across the country.

Key Milestones

2000

19th Feb. 2000: The Foundation Stone of the institution was laid down

2000

2nd October 2000: The Mahatma Gandhi Hospital was formally inaugurated on the birth anniversary of the Father of the Nation - Mahatma Gandhi and Former Prime Minister of India – Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri.

2001

Establishment of ‘Mahatma Gandhi Medical College, Jaipur’ and the first batch of 100 MBBS Students admitted after permission from MCI and GOI.

2003

Establishment of ‘Mahatma Gandhi Dental College & Hospital’ and ‘Mahatma Gandhi Nursing College’.

2005

Establishment of ‘Mahatma Gandhi Physiotherapy College’

2011

Establishment of ‘Mahatma Gandhi University of Medical Sciences and Technology, Jaipur’

2014

Establishment of ‘Mahatma Gandhi University of Occupational Therapy College’

2019

Establishment of ‘Mahatma Gandhi College of Hospital Administration’ and ‘Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Health Informatics, Jaipur’

2022

Establishment of ‘Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Allied Health Sciences’

As MGUMST expanded its horizons, it continued to achieve milestones in medical education and research. The university's commitment to excellence led to collaborations with renowned institutions, accreditation from relevant bodies, and the introduction of innovative programs to meet the evolving needs of the healthcare sector.

Mahatma Gandhi University of Medical Sciences and Technology stands as a testament to its founders' vision and dedication. With a focus on producing skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals, conducting ground-breaking research, and addressing societal health challenges, MGUMST looks toward the future with a commitment to advancing medical sciences and technology.

The history of Mahatma Gandhi University of Medical Sciences and Technology is a narrative of growth, integration, and unwavering commitment to the ideals of Mahatma Gandhi. From its inception as a hospital to the establishment of a renowned university, MGUMST continues to play a pivotal role in shaping the landscape of medical education, research, and healthcare services in Jaipur and beyond.

India Education Trust

India Education Trust is the sponsoring body of Mahatma Gandhi University of Medical Sciences and Technology, Jaipur. It was established as a Public Charitable Trust and is duly registered with Devasthan Department, Government of Rajasthan under Rajasthan Public Trust Act, 1959 (Act No. 42 of 1959) vide Registration No. 20/Jaipur/1999 dated May 24, 1999. The Trust is also registered with the Income Tax Department under Section 11 of Income Tax Act, 1961 and is exempted from tax. The office of the India Education Trust is at RIICO Institutional Area, Sitapura, Tonk Road, Jaipur-302022.

The main objectives of the Trust are (a) to undertake research and studies in Medical, Dental, Nursing, Physiotherapy and related disciplines (b) to develop educational institutions conducting studies and research in these disciplines (c) to continuously improve the health status of people by providing primary as well as advanced health care (d) to establish hospitals including specialty and super-specialty clinics, dispensaries, maternity homes and RCH centres (e) to develop health manpower by creating training centres for medical and allied health care professionals and to promote and support activities of other educational institutions which are catering and improving public health at large.
